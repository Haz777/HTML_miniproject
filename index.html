Monuments & Men

Monuments make momentous men immortal,
but more memorable are mortal men,
making mere moments immortal.

Joseph

Salmon & Kippers

Frank was frying bigger fish -
claimed he had no time for kippers;

'Salmon is a finer fish'
Yet, oft he longed for kippers.

Gordon

Boy & Universe

Somewhere in this vast universe,
There grows a boy.

And somewhere in this boy,
There grows a vast universe.

Levitt
